    BattleBoats.io

    Compete within the battles of BattleBoats IO, the favored Online multi-player io game. Join a Team and shoot down enemy boats and cannons. Destroy your opponent's headquarters with all the penetration of your team's Mine. Collect the grey mine symbols on the map. you'll also collect experience boxes with which you'll level up or repair boxes and other useful stuff. If your ship is destroyed, you'll need to wait a couple of seconds before you'll join the battle again. So avoid being shot down at any cost! Lead your team to victory on the ocean.
