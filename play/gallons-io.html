    Gallons.io

    Gallons.io is a web multiplayer IO game, within the water. A valuable natural element that has been taken without any consideration for hundreds of years. during this game you've got the goal to gather the maximum amount water as possible. a bit like all the opposite players who want to become as rich and powerful as you. you've got to put pumps and pipes near water sources to earn money. Buy cool Upgrades and weapons to defend your headquarters. Because if someone destroys it, you've lost.
