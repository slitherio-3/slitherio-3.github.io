    Squadd.io

    Are you uninterested in these hyper-realistic shooters where you hop around sort of a frog and fire fully automatic weapons at your opponents? In Squadd.IO are you able to enjoy the simplest Element of person shooters without having to endure the annoying sides. check out your environment from an isometric point of view and see where your attackers are shooting from. Collect items and level up to enhance your skills. Join Team Blue or Team Red and fight together. Especially enjoy Pixel-based block graphics like old-school drawing styles. So, reload and obtain started! in any case, there are still people in Squadd.IO to shoot to pieces.
