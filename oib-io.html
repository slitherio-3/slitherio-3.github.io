    Oib.io

    Time for a replacement, cool online multiplayer io game. In Oib.io you'll start with one among the various bosses with different abilities. together with your character, you'll generate a touch Oibs. the small Oibs are like tiny soldiers fighting for you. you'll assemble them into an outsized Oib or divide them into many small soldiers. you'll also feed them to form them stronger. Don't stop generating more and more Oibs and keep an eye fixed out for other players to attack. Sounds cool, doesn't it on Oib.io.
