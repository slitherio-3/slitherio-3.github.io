    Bigfoxwarz.io

    Bigfoxwarz.io is another fun addicting online IO game about becoming the most important player on the sector. Enter a nickname and control a small little fox on a forest full of evil enemies. Your goal is to eat fruits and smaller players so as to grow. As a fox, you'll use holes to run from larger foxes who will attempt to catch you, and instantly reappear on another a part of the sector. Don't stop eating and take your name to the highest of the scoreboard with BigfoxWarz.io
