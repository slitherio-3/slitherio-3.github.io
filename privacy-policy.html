    What Personal Data We Collect and Why
    Comments
    When visitors leave comments on the site we collect the data shown on the comment form, and also to help detect the visitor's IP address and browser user agent string spam.

    An anonymous string created from your email address (also called hash) can be provided to the Gravatar service to see if you are using it. Gravatar service privacy policy is available here: https://automattic.com/privacy/. After your comment has been approved, your profile picture is public in the context of your comment.

    Environment
    If you upload images to a website, you should avoid uploading images containing embedded location data (EXIF GPS). Website visitors download and extract data anywhere in images on the website.

    Contact Forms
    Cookies
    If you leave a comment on our site, you may choose to save your name, email address and website to cookies. When you leave another comment, they are for your convenience so you don't have to fill out your information again. These cookies will last a year.

    If you have an account and log in to it, we will set a temporary cookie to determine whether your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

    When you sign in, we'll also set up a few cookies to save your login information and screenshot options. Login cookies last two days and screen options cookies last a year. If you choose "Remember Me," your entry will continue for two weeks. If you log out of your account, login cookies will be removed.

    If you edit or publish an article, an additional cookie will be saved in your browser. This cookie does not contain any personal data and only shows the post identity of the article you edited. It ends in a day.

    Embedded content from other Websites
    Articles on this site may contain embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves the same way as a visitor visits the other Web site.

    These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party monitoring, and monitor your interaction with embedded content, including if you have an account and you have signed in to this website, including monitoring your interaction with embedded content.

    How Long Do We Store Your Data
    If you leave a comment, the description and metadata are protected indefinitely. Therefore, we can automatically recognize and approve any follow-up comment instead of keeping it in a control queue.

    For users who register on our website (if any), we also store the personal information they provide on their user profile. All users can see, edit or delete their personal information at any time (they cannot change their usernames). Website administrators can also see and edit this information.

    What Rights Do You Have On Your Data
    If you have an account on this site or have not commented, you may request to receive an exported file of personal data that we hold about you, including the data you provide to us. You can also request that we delete the personal data we hold about you. This does not contain any data that we must store for administrative, legal, or security purposes.
